Pani Puri

Prep Time: 20mins || Cooking Time: 15mins

Ingredients

  1. Puris or Golgappa (ready-made or homemade)
  2. 1 finely chopped Onion
  3. 1/2 cup Sev
  4. 1/4 cup Date Tamarind Chutney

For Pani

  1. 1/2 cup Mint Leaves
  2. 1/2 cup Coriander Leaves
  3. 1-2 Green Chilli
  4. 1/2 inch pieces of Ginger
  5. 1½ medium size Lemon
  6. 3 tablespoons Sugar
  7. 3 tablespoons Sugar
  8. 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder
  9. 1/4 teaspoons Black Salt
  10. Salt to taste
  11. 4 cups of water

For Masala

  1. 1½ cups boiled, peeled and mashed Potato
  2. 1/2 cup boiled Kala Chana
  3. 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
  4. 1/2 teaspoon Cumin-Coriander Powder
  5. 1/4 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder
  6. 2 tablespoons finely chopped Coriander Leaves
  7. Salt to taste

Making Pani
Rinse coriander and mint leaves in water and take all the ingredients of pani.
Add mint leaves, coriander leaves, green chilli, ginger and lemon juice in the small chutney jar of a grinder,
Grind until smooth paste (if required, add 1/4 cup water while grinding).
Transfer them to a large bowl and add sugar, chaat masala powder, black salt and 4 cups water. Stir with a large spoon and mix it properly. Pani is ready;

Making Masala
Take mashed potato, kala chana, red chilli powder, cumin-coriander powder, chaat masala powder, coriander leaves and salt.
Mix them together with a spoon. Masala is ready.

Assemble Pani Pur
Take each puri and gently make a large hole on its top-middle side.
Stuff it with masala. Sprinkle onion and sev over it and drizzle a drop of date tamarind chutney over it. Take pani-puri water in a medium bowl.
Dip each puri in water and enjoy…
